Overview

Welcome to ECON 317 — Economic Development! Today’s lesson will be an overview of the content and the assignments of the course. Please read and familiarize yourself with the syllabus.

This is not just a “syllabus day,” as we need to hit the ground running, but today will give you a “big picture” look at this semester by starting with some of the biggest questions, not just in “development economics” or “economics,” but arguably all of social science:

  1. Why are some countries wealthy, and others poor?
  2. How did/do countries become wealthy?

We then also consider the history “development economics” as a self-identifying field of economics, as compared to the original motivations for economics or political economy (in Adam Smith’s time) as a discipline.
